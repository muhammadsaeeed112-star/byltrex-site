Frequently Asked

Questions about
missed call recovery.

How many patients do clinics lose from missed calls? +

Dental clinics miss 20–35% of incoming calls during business hours and nearly 100% after hours. Each missed call represents $500–$1,200 in lost treatment revenue, meaning most practices lose $15,000–$50,000+ monthly from unanswered calls alone.

How does the AI missed call solution work? +

When a call goes unanswered, the AI system instantly engages the caller via text or callback, answers common questions about services, insurance, and hours, and books appointments directly into your practice management software — all without staff involvement.

Can the AI handle dental-specific questions? +

Yes. The AI is trained on dental terminology, common procedures, insurance verification workflows, and emergency triage protocols. It can answer questions about cleanings, crowns, implants, Invisalign, accepted insurance plans, and route urgent cases appropriately.

Does it integrate with my dental software? +

Absolutely. We integrate with Dentrix, Eaglesoft, Open Dental, Curve Dental, and any practice management system with an API. Appointments booked by the AI sync directly to your schedule in real time.

What about lunch breaks and after hours? +

The AI handles every missed call 24/7/365 — during lunch breaks, after hours, weekends, and holidays. Callers never hit voicemail. Instead they get an instant response from an AI that books appointments at 2 AM just as effectively as 2 PM.

How fast is setup? +

Most dental clinics are fully operational within 5–7 business days. We handle setup, integrate with your PMS, train the AI on your specific services and protocols, and run test scenarios before going live. No technical expertise required from your team.
