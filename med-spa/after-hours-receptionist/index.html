Frequently Asked

Questions med spa owners
actually ask.

How does the after-hours AI receptionist work? +

The AI activates automatically when your front desk closes. It answers every call with your brand voice, books consultations into your scheduling software, answers treatment questions, and sends SMS confirmations — all without human involvement. Your team arrives to a full overnight report.

What hours does it cover? +

Any hours you need — evenings, nights, weekends, holidays, or 24/7. Most med spas activate it from 6pm to 9am on weekdays and all day on weekends, but it's fully customizable to your schedule.

Does it integrate with my booking software? +

Yes. We integrate with Meevo, Vagaro, Mindbody, Zenoti, Mangomint, Boulevard, and any platform with an API. The AI books directly into your live schedule — no manual entry in the morning.

How much does it cost? +

Typically $499 to $1,499 per month depending on call volume and integration depth. Most med spas recover the cost from a single after-hours booking — a Botox consultation alone is worth $2,000+.

What if a caller has a medical concern? +

The AI is trained to identify medical concerns. It provides appropriate guidance, offers to connect with on-call staff if available, and flags the concern for immediate follow-up when your team opens.

How quickly can it be set up? +

5 to 7 business days from discovery call to live deployment. We learn your treatments, providers, after-hours protocols, and brand voice, then build, train, and stress-test.
